We Can Use Faith

May 18th 2024 Justine 3 Min read

Today I am testifying wondrous deeds of our Lord. Fear gripped my heart for so long, tightening its hold with each passing day. But today, I am here, finally able to testify and say, 'Thank you, Lord.'

In the depths of fear, there come a day when it seemed overwhelming when I felt it reach its peak. In that moment, I braced myself for the worst, for in every circumstance, it seemed there was a reason for despair. I prayed eagerly, tears streaming down my face, crying out to the heavens, 'Oh Lord, I beseech you, hear my plea'

And indeed, the lord heard me. His grace enveloped me, His mercy washed over me like a soothing balm. For this I am eternally grateful. As it is written in Psalm 34:4-5

I sought the Lord, and he answered me and deliverd me from all my fears. Those who look to him are radiant, and their faces shall never be ashamed. Psalm 34:4-5

Today, I stand not in the shadow of fear, but in the light of gratitude.

Be still, and know that I am God. Psalm 46:10
